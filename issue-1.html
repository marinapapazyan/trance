Editors' Note


Trance Magazine is a platform for urgent, visceral and cathartic literature.
 
The magazine treats writing as a practice of purification and exploration of the unconscious.
 
A noise. An unclear noise. A continuous unconscious noise. The theme murmur stands for a whisper needing an amplifier.
 
“You are not an outsider, you are an artist graduating from an Art institution...” My mentor once told me over a pile of angrily typed love letters. “...As they are, these texts are not art, they are the vomit of a madman. ”
 
Are impulsive, cathartic and therapeutic texts bound to stay in the drawer of the “artist”, unless to be given value through transformation into “High Art”; designed, re-made into objects of easy consumption?
 
Ironically (but not so ironically), our intention with Trance Magazine is and always will be to value such texts written out of urgency, emotion, impulse; in their original, authentic, “un-digested” form, without the need for transformation, translation, simplification, illustration...
 
Trance Magazine, with its first issue, wants to enable a space for voices to reach a larger audience, a space where voices are not trembling, voices are not murmurs.
