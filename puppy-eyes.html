Puppy Eyes

published in Issue I: Murmur
 
 
For a passing moment
we know what we know
no need to put on a fucking funny show.
 
I know the drill
nevermind the thrill.
 
Puppy eyes
looking at me all bright
you wanna fuck me
alright
then treat me right. (...)
