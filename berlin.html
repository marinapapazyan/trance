Lui Macrae

Berlin- Neither here nor there

published in Issue II: Nerves
 
 
We travel the nervous system
Of a metropolis built on pain and shame
Another mass merging with the web of lies
The web of lives
A virtual sameness a Primark, a Macdonalds and a H&M
Hell & Man — are now synonymous
Symptomless
 
Concrete atoms & Concave temples
Cower before the eye of the past
The architect designed these slabs to bleed
Red LEDs, Red bodies, Red flames
Your torso is a shape
Penetrated
disseminated
Repurposed and uncompensated
Sweat — a toxic ventilation
Lust ran deep
Forms melted into singular form
We’d arrived in a rift
Of constant transition
On the brink of a full translation
