Asia is such an exotic place
Yoga in the morning
Falling asleep wearing kimono
 
Oh, maybe i should eat sushi tomorrow

Fun fun fun

Swinging their fists

Now we are hippies and politicians
Feel like whole world is looking at me
World is such a weird place
Just stand here
complain complain complain
Until it makes you feel powerful
