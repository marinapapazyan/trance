Three Visits

a requiem

published in Issue II: Nerves
 
 
In the waving grass
and under passing clouds
there is a place without a crowd
where I can go to
any given day
a little private moment
no talking today
the closest place to heaven
that I have ever been
if there is a place on earth
it’s here she can be seen
 
and I think it is the day
for all that I hold dear
The noises and the rubble
gently fade away
I stand still and see it happen
Everything gets clear
and with me all my troubles
silently disappear
now there seems no difference
between happiness and fear
 
I walk the forrest’s nerves
and their lights shine through my mind
the dark in and all around
is turning neutral and rewind
strangely familiar present
first time I found
its a state of mind I’ve never been
like a different deliverance
as if it were a dream
 
both in and around me
it offers me no choice
it shivers through me
I can hear her voice
not a single worry
nothing she preserves
cleaned my ears with certainty
stillness she assures
cleanses and whispers
blessings in my ears
mother nature’s clarity
wash my head with tears
 
now one single pine leaf
assures me I can say
the way as it has been since birth
is the peace thats in me
and will never go away
the wind that blows around me
is showing me the way
recon that it bares her face
and I know that it will stay
a sight so immaculately clean
where in my eyes
the blood stains
bluntly turn to cream
 
all four seasons
in one single glance
close my eyes
my pupils stare in trance
it seems so great and empty
my heart skips a beat
behold all that is left to fill
the hilltop is my seat
the streets are all sweaty
the asphalt stains my feet
given a path that’s woven
so perfectly
while the water here is frozen
the sun touches my lips
the vast field slips open
some thoughts grow weary
and other hearts get broken
all of that is too far
from me away
I’m just like that nature
taking it day by day
 
my nose with your smells
is dancing in the sky
my thoughts seemed so confused
but now I cant remember why
in this place I am with you
and I am here too
smiling
breathing
dying
been that way
my whole life long
and will be that way
till the day I die
and thats all okay
just remembered why
more than ever I am living now
every single day
I only have to try
 
I take the same old path again
but this time in reverse
although I’ve been walking this way
for at least while
the way that I see it now
is another first
it makes every inch
feel as long as a feet
so I am walking right beside you now
in this place where we always used
and will always meet
your moss and sands gently pet my feet
and so for the coming while
you bring on my face
your ever-loving smile
 
and together
we go on
always
and always
with that smile
we will walk along
