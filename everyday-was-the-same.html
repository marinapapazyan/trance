Everyday was the same
One day I decided to learn a recipe
by Gennaro Contaldo.
I saw this recipe on a Jamie Oliver Youtube video.
It required a few ingredients.
Olive oil, spaghetti, garlic, chilli, sausages, salt and pepper.
I didn’t really strictly follow the recipe but I understood what I had to do.
There were still steps I had to follow,
and I haven’t tried to change anything since then. (...)
