ABOUT ARCHIVE ISSUES OPEN CALL SHOP

Trance magazine is a platform for urgent and cathartic literature.
We aim to create a space for visceral reading experiences as well as dialogues for writers and readers alike.
 
For the launch of each issue, we organise performative events where writers can play with new ways of bringing their texts to life.
